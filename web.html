What Exactly Is UNICEF?

Firstly, UNICEF is known as United Nations Children's Fund. Second, let's talk about what the mission of UNICEF is. UNICEF stands for every child, everywhere, and therefore our mission is to mobilize and empower people around the world to invest in the positive transformation of every child's future.

Yearly Plan

Activity 1(Lunar New Year Bazaar): Brownie bits stall

Come and enjoy some delicious brownies in a cute container. The money donated will be given to the charity. So make sure to buy those goodness!

Activity 2(Open Day): Booth game

What do you think of when hearing the name of booth games? Well, if you love throwing rings, then do come and enjoy our game. Show us your skills and win some prizes!

Activity 3(OLE Day): Cheesecake Cupcakes

Baking some delicious cheesecakes would lessen the stressful life of our students as well as give them some knowledge about baking.

Activity 4(OLE Day): Ocean Park Visit

We understand our students love themeparks. Especially after a long time of exams period, who wouldn't want to relax and play some games?

Activity 5: Orphanage Visit

Orphans are adorable people, so let's spend some time and play games with them! This way, we will all have a quality day and will brighten up the days of the orphans as well.

Activity 6: Soap Making

Mmhhmhm, soap smells great honestly, do come and try joining our workshop so that you can make your own kind of soap!
